How to Use

  • Click "Start Camera" to activate the scanner

Having Issues?

  • Make sure camera permission is granted
  • Ensure good lighting for better scanning
  • Hold the device steady while scanning
  • Try switching cameras if available
  • Refresh the page if scanner stops working
